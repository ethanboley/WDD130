Overview

Purpose

Help people enjoy a magical rafting experience. Which includes getting those interested a good intro into how to raft effectively to the point where rafting on land is even a viable option. We will also help those interested by directing them to fun rafting destinations and places to find good, strong, and powerful rafts lying around as well as their effective propellant, the Korok Leaf.

Audience

It doesn't matter what region you hale from or what species you are, everyone in the world needs to know how to use, where to find and take a raft. it doesn't matter if you're seven and going on an adventure with your parents; seventeen and going off to see the world; fifty-seven and having an existencial crisis; or one-hundred seventeen and tasked by the ghost of a dead king to save the world. Whatever the case may be, this site will be there to Help. This site is to target a wide range of financial situations by providing information about affordable rafting. folk are often at a loss on the details of rafting and this site will be an easilly accessible way to educate them in this new and exciting world. As it is generally a more casual site, the site will likely be accessed via mobile or personal devices ranging from Laptops to Sheikah Slates to Mobile Phones.
