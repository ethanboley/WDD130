Overview

Purpose

I love climbing, I'm a rock climber, I enjoy finding fun and challenging climbs. My company helps people like you find those climbs. My company finds fun places to climb both indoor and outdoor, using your feedback. This results in an up to date, list of all the best and most popular spots to have a good time. Whether you're an outdoor or an indoor climber you may have experienced a point where you didn't know where to go next. That's where I come in, my website helps you never have a dull moment by seeing the best places to go in real time and even all the tips and tricks of previous climbers. I do this because I love it and I want new and veteran climbers to have a good time wherever you go.

Audience

Climbing is a very versatile sport as it has many different difficulties. For this reason the audience for this website is basically everyone with at least a couple limbs, a brain and a desire to either start a new hobby or even just to have a one time adventure. I for one have seen in my life a surprisingly diverse culture of climbers and I Love it. I've seen a blind person climb a parapalegic climb (better than me as it turns out), I've seen ten year old world champs and ordinary folk like myself. So, really, there will be a very diverse group of people who may visit this site.

Content

Dyno Home page: Where to climb

Here on the main page there will be an on site (interactive) map of the surrounding neighborhood with good climbing locations pre-pinned and ready to navigate to. Visitors need only to know where to find the site and to know what the site offers. For this reason the links and buttons clearly see from the main page and also some text surrounding them will clearly define the page content and exactly what it offers. Why come to this site over another? All other sites are trying to sell something like the home site of a local climbing gym for example, they're biased. Not me, I just like the sport and enjoy spending time helping others feel the same about it. So basically this site will be a good general hub for climbers all over the place to come and have a good and easy time enjoying the sport. When folk open up the site they will see something pretty simple, just a navigation bar on the top of the page that will stay at the top of the page and help the folks navigate the website more easily this will be followed by a large stock looking image of a climbing gym right next to a similarly stock-ish image of a mountain cliff side with folks climbing on it likely both images will be of lead climbing. These images will have buttons on them with descriptions below them which will navigate them to different parts of that same page which will have details about the respective button (outdoor or indoor). This home page other than that will have links to the subpages in the header and links to the media and contact information in the footer. Lower home page. These lower sections of the home page will have links to and information about climbing gyms and their prices and other things like that. Further down will be outdoor climbing locations and their respective ratings and reviews.

How to climb

A good part of my site will be dedicated to educating folks on climbing and how and what to look for in climbing as it pertains to their level of experience and skill so they can know what to do, where to go, and how to have the most fun while climbing. The other parts of the site will be dedicated to making finding good locations easy and convenient. Page 2 will be all about basically educating people about climbing and terms and such. This page will, similar to the home page, have links to the subpages and the home page in the header and links to the home page and this page will have a very simple footer with probably just copyright info and contact info amid maybe a different colored background. This page will also have things like fun facts and links to videos of cool or inspiring or otherwise climbing related climbing there may be a video from the tokyo olympics climbing events or likely the world championship climbers, another about that one 10 year old girl who was crushing records and there will also be videos that will act as quick tutorials for those newer who wish to get into climbing, other things like that.
